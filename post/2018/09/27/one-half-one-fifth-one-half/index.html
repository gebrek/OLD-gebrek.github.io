One Half One Fifth One Half

Jacob Sonnenberg

2018/09/27

Tags: nil

It has been about four weeks since the start of the period, and the semester. The classes have been alright, although I had to drop the one I was really interested in: Lambda Calculus. Not sure if I'll ever have a satisfying class on the topics of Lisp and its foundations.

Protocol Validation is alright, it's got a good approach to the topics covered. Mu-calculus is up my alley, the way of expressing process is simple yet expressive. It's a bit like logic programming. Although the subject concerns imperative processes, the mode of expression seems opposed to such linear thoughts. When working in it, one should consider sets and families of processes, rather than a particular implementation of one form of process. In this it's useful for abstracting procedures and talking about them in generically applicable ways. Quite interesting, but I've still a ways to go before I really grok it.

We have a homework assignment due late October we're meant to pair up for, but I dawdled on that and may be one of the only people left without a partner...
