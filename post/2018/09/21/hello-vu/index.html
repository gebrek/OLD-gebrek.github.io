Hello Vu

Jacob Sonnenberg

2018/09/21

Tags: nil

One month ago, on August 20 I landed in the Netherlands to do a Master degree at the VU Amsterdam. It's been a journey and already I can tell the experience will be well worth living. My flat is filled with 12 students, all of whom seem to be good company. If I recall, 4 are Master students the rest are Bachelors.

My neighbor at +1 is a hoot. He's an Estonian studying Mathematics. Has plenty of opinions on technologies, a true Linux zealot. More low level interests though, despite the major mathematics. Likes to say he prefers to study what he knows least.

My neighbor at -1 is an American, although for the past few years he has been living in Hong Kong. He studies Organizational Psychology. He can be a bit crass and brash, but he's got a good thing going on. The same age as me but well traveled.

In the last week we got a pool table for the floor! It's pretty rad. We all pitched in to get one. It seems to be a great addition.
