For love of text

A strange language we’ve made, that lives between mind, tongue, and eye. From the odd realm of the mind that does not seem to make sense to itself, free-form neuronal firings formed into still ephemeral utterances, then cast into text, made permenent.

What of these is more real? More true? Where does the thought lie?

The answer as it appears to me… is unimportant really. The act matters more. Each level of forming thought develops the thought more. In the cradle of the mind live half-formed conceptions, made more real, more thoroughly articulated by speech and writing.

That’s the intended exercise, to manifest the slight and ephemeral.
